EXAMPLE CITY POLICE DEPARTMENT — ARREST/INCIDENT REPORT
Bates: USAO-000001 to USAO-000006
Reporting Officer: D. Kowalski, Badge #4471
Date/Time of incident: January 14, 2026, approx. 21:40

NARRATIVE:

On the above date I was on patrol when I observed a gray sedan traveling
eastbound. I initiated a traffic stop because a tree-shaped air freshener was
hanging from the rearview mirror, which I believed obstructed the driver's view
in violation of municipal code.

The driver, identified as Marcus DOE, appeared nervous. I requested a K-9 unit.
While awaiting the K-9 unit, DOE remained detained at the roadside for
approximately 35 minutes. The K-9 unit arrived and alerted to the vehicle.

DOE gave verbal consent to search the vehicle. During the search I located a
plastic bag containing a white rock-like substance under the front passenger
seat, and a Smith & Wesson 9mm pistol in the glove compartment.

A 911 call earlier that evening reporting a "man with a gun" in the area is
documented separately (see dispatch recording). Officer L. Reyes arrived as a
backup unit and his body-worn camera was also activated.

My own body-worn camera (BWC-001) recorded the stop and search.

DOE was placed under arrest and transported to Central Booking.
