# Case Memory — United States v. Marcus Doe — No. 25-CR-00417

_Last updated: 2026-06-18. First pass by Criminal Discovery Assistant — for attorney review. Local files only._

## Charges

**Count One — Distribution of a Controlled Substance, 21 U.S.C. § 841(a)(1)** (on/about 1/14/2026, cocaine base).
Elements: (1) knowing/intentional (2) distribution (3) of a controlled substance.

**Count Two — Felon in Possession of a Firearm, 18 U.S.C. § 922(g)(1)** (on/about 1/14/2026, S&W 9mm).
Elements: (1) prior felony conviction; (2) knowledge of that status; (3) knowing possession of a firearm; (4) interstate-commerce nexus.

## The government's theory

A traffic stop of a gray sedan driven by Doe, prompted in part by a CI tip and a 911 "man with a gun" call, led to a K-9 alert and a vehicle search that recovered 6.2 g of crack cocaine (passenger seat) and a 9mm pistol (glovebox). The lab confirms the substance. The government treats the recovered drugs as supporting **distribution** and the pistol as the **§ 922(g)** firearm.

## Evidence by count

**Count One (distribution):**
- Supports: lab report confirms 6.2 g cocaine base (USAO-000010); drugs recovered from the car Doe was driving (USAO-000001).
- Undercuts: **No distribution evidence produced** — no controlled buy, scales, packaging, ledgers, currency, or buyer. Quantity (6.2 g) is consistent with possession. Jane Roe says a *different* man placed the bag (USAO-000013).
- Key files: USAO-000010 (lab), USAO-000001 (arrest), USAO-000013 (witness).

**Count Two (felon in possession):**
- Supports: pistol recovered from glovebox (USAO-000001, USAO-000007).
- Undercuts: possession is constructive only (car, not person); Jane Roe "did not see anyone with a gun"; prior-felony predicate and knowledge-of-status not yet documented in this production.
- Key files: USAO-000001, USAO-000007, USAO-000013.

## Flags to investigate

- **Exculpatory / Brady-Giglio:**
  - Jane Roe statement (USAO-000013) — third party placed the drugs; Doe is not the person she saw. Goes to both counts.
  - CI reliability/payment documentation withheld (USAO-000007) — Giglio / possible Franks issue.
  - Lab analyst proficiency-test history and instrument calibration logs not provided (USAO-000010) — Giglio / Daubert.
- **Suppression hooks (investigate, not adjudicated):**
  - Pretextual stop on air-freshener obstruction (USAO-000001).
  - ~35-minute roadside detention awaiting K-9 — prolonged-detention / *Rodriguez v. United States* issue.
  - Consent voluntariness after prolonged detention; sequence vs. K-9 alert unclear.
- **Inconsistencies:**
  - Search basis conflict: Kowalski reports a **consent search** (USAO-000001); Reyes reports search **incident to arrest** (USAO-000007). Different legal justifications for the same search.
