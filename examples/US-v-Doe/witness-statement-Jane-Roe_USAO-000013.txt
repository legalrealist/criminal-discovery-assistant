WITNESS STATEMENT
Bates: USAO-000013 to USAO-000014
Witness: Jane Roe
Date: January 14, 2026

I was standing near the corner when the police pulled over the gray car. Earlier
that evening, before the stop, I saw a different man — not the driver who was
arrested — open the passenger door of that same car and put a small bag under
the seat. He then walked away. The man the police arrested was not the person I
saw place the bag in the car.

I did not see anyone with a gun.

Signed, Jane Roe
