INDICTMENT

The Grand Jury charges that:

COUNT ONE
(Distribution of a Controlled Substance — 21 U.S.C. § 841(a)(1))

On or about January 14, 2026, in the District of Example, the defendant
MARCUS DOE did knowingly and intentionally distribute a mixture and substance
containing a detectable amount of cocaine base ("crack cocaine"), a Schedule II
controlled substance.

COUNT TWO
(Felon in Possession of a Firearm — 18 U.S.C. § 922(g)(1))

On or about January 14, 2026, in the District of Example, the defendant
MARCUS DOE, knowing he had previously been convicted of a crime punishable by
imprisonment for a term exceeding one year, did knowingly possess a firearm,
namely a Smith & Wesson 9mm pistol, said firearm having been shipped and
transported in interstate commerce.

A TRUE BILL.
